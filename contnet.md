THE PROBLEM (2 columns, conversational)

The way we get paid is broken.

You finish the work on Tuesday. The wire arrives on Friday — if you're lucky. Meanwhile rent is due, groceries need buying, and you've already started the next project.

Or you're the one doing the hiring. You pay upfront and hope. You pay at the end and feel guilty watching someone wait. There's no good option.

And if you've ever tried doing any of this on-chain — your salary, your contractor rate, your client's name — it's all public. Anyone with a browser can see it.

StreamLine fixes all three.


HOW IT WORKS

Money flows as you work.

Lock the full amount once. Define your milestones. Hit a checkpoint — client approves — and the stream opens. From that moment, money drips into your wallet continuously. Every minute. Every hour. While you sleep.

No invoice. No waiting. No chasing.


Three steps. That's it.

1. Lock
Client locks the full amount on-chain. Funds are committed — they can't disappear. You start working knowing the money is there.

2. Drip
The moment a milestone is approved, streaming starts. USDC arrives in your wallet in real time — every few minutes, automatically, gaslessly.

3. Done
All milestones complete, stream exhausted. Clean on-chain record. No loose ends.


If the client goes quiet?

After 48 hours of silence, the stream auto-approves. Silence is not a veto. You can't be ghosted out of your payment.
